The Book

STARING.

A call to awareness and action

In a world that moves at breakneck speed, "STARING." invites you to slow down, observe, and truly see. This collection of poems serves as both mirror and magnifying glass, reflecting our inner landscapes while examining the complexities of modern existence.

Through vivid imagery and thoughtful verse, TGN (COSSOE) Howard challenges readers to move beyond passive observation to active engagement with the world around them.
